About Me.

“The nitrogen in our DNA, the calcium in our teeth, the iron in our blood, the carbon in our apple pies were made in the interiors of collapsing stars. We are made of star-stuff.” ― Carl Sagan, Cosmos

Like the vast and ever-expanding cosmos, the fields of data science are in a state of continuous expansion, which both fascinates me.

My education has provided me with the foundations for engaging with computers, numbers, and programming languages confidently. My experience in the area of technology and data analysis has allowed me to incorporate my learning towards solving real-world challenges. I want to explore and satisfy my interest in the world of Big Data, predictive analytics, and AI while developing solutions to some of the challenges faced by society and companies. My experience as a data analyst intern on big, data-intensive projects in Tamil Nadu, India has given me a strong exposure to the real-world problems that different companies and consumers are facing. Nowadays, it’s essential to comprehend and process all different types of data and to evaluate it to arrive at the right option for decision-making as there are infinite possibilities.

I find the data collection, analysis, and visualization processes fulfilling as they provide fresh insights from converting the raw data into significant information to help make weighted decisions. I have analyzed data and have ample hands-on experience in programming languages, related data packages (like Python, Python - Pandas, Scipy, Scikit, etc.) and making graphs using Excel and Tableau.

Building mission-critical, data-driven systems that can address challenging concerns has enhanced and strengthened my learning as a data analyst and has also reinforced my knowledge in the field of Data Science. My knowledge and practice have made me aware of different data processing options, tools, and practices.

My ambition is to be a Data Scientist in an organization gaining all the knowledge and later build my enterprise focusing on mentoring a new generation of data scientists focusing on innovations to solve real-world issues. By applying the most successful utilization of data analytics and predictive approaches to maximize business profitability, I intend to help organizations make accurate, data-driven decisions based on trends in key metrics.
